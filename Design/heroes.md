Skill Trees
Rarely branching, available at different levels, many skills not all can combine with other heroes' skills for combo attack.  Skill branches will naturally lead to different path choices and are purchased with skill points earned during battle.  At resolution phase, skills become available and you must spend skill points on them.  

Passives
Heroes gain new passives which increase with level like stats.  Passives automatically form synergies when present with other heroes passives.  Example Heroes Courage(Knight + Knight) As the relationship itself levels up the synergy becomes strengthened.  Is there a difference between synergy and relationship(I forget). I believe relationships are based on hero class and synergies are based on tags. New personal passives are gained automatically at certain levels(hero or relationship) and do not have to be purchased with skill points.
